A RPG using OOP in Javascript!

Choose your character!

Warrior

Warriors have higher health and stamina, however their strenght also makes them slower and clumsier

Rogue

Rogues are fast and have high stamina and speed which makes up for their lack in strength and health.

Mage

Mages are amazing spell casters. But they weak in health which basically makes them glass gannons.

Hunter

Hunters are well rounded fighters that focuses on evening out their skills. No major weaknesses or strengths.
